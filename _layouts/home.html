About

(because she has braces, get it?)

Ana Ortez-Rivera is a first-year MPP candidate at the Harvard Kennedy School. She has stepped up to the plate to run for this year’s VP of Internal Affairs.

Ana is ready to dedicate her career to public service but first is motivated to serve the community at HKS and make a difference. How? By listening to her peers, advocating for change, and being a loud voice that the administration can’t ignore.

What exactly will be she doing as the VP of Internal Affairs? The role entails:

Serving as the primary liaison for official HKS student organizations and works with the Office of Student Services to support these organizations.

Ana will go beyond the role and meet the needs of students and HKS organizations. She will also work internally with other roles and committees to meet her platform. She will fight for:
